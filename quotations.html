Yet her smell, it doth linger Painting pictures in my mind. 
Her eyes, bowls of honey Angel's harps shall laughs. 
Oh lark, grant a sign If crooked be Cupid's shaft. 
Hark! The lark! Her name it hath spake. 
"Cecily," it discharges 
From twixt its wee beak.
